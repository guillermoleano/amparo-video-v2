# Storytelling Guide — Comercial Amparo

**Title:** Todos necesitamos un Amparo.
**Duration:** 60–90 seconds

*Fuente: página "RESOURCES" del Notion del proyecto (AMPARO PROJECT → AMPARO). Guardado acá como insumo base para el agente de guion.*

---

## Objective

This commercial should emotionally connect with insurance advisors by showing that Amparo is not just software, but a trusted companion that helps them regain control of their time and focus on what truly matters.

The audience should finish the video thinking: **"I need an Amparo."**

---

## Narrative Structure

The commercial must always follow these ten scenes.

---

### Scene 1 — An Impossible Day

Time: 8:00 AM. Fast-paced editing. The insurance advisor opens their laptop. WhatsApp is overflowing. Messages arrive continuously.

Examples:
- "¿Ya revisaste mi póliza?"
- "Necesito mi certificado."
- "¿Me puedes responder?"
- "Es urgente."

The phone vibrates constantly. Email fills up. Clients call simultaneously. For every message answered, five more arrive. The advisor becomes visibly overwhelmed. Coffee gets cold. The office feels increasingly chaotic. Music intensifies.

**Emotion:** Stress. Overload. Anxiety.

---

### Scene 2 — Customers Become Frustrated

Several customers appear. One waits. Another sends another message. Another becomes impatient.

Examples:
- "¿Sigues ahí?"
- "No me respondes."
- "Voy a buscar otro asesor."

Meanwhile the advisor only thinks: "I need help..."

**Emotion:** Frustration.

---

### Scene 3 — Burnout

Night. The office is empty. Only the advisor remains. He looks at an endless list of tasks. Sighs. Lays his head on the desk. Falls asleep. Silence.

**Emotion:** Exhaustion.

---

### Scene 4 — The Dream

Everything changes. The office disappears. Now the advisor walks through a Colombian cloud forest. Birds. Wind. Water. Soft cinematic music. Mist.

Between the trees appears a silhouette. It is a spectacled bear. The bear walks calmly. He smiles. He wears a small tie or elegant briefcase. He never feels threatening. He represents calm. Hope. Trust.

---

### Scene 5 — The Encounter

The advisor watches in surprise. The bear approaches. Offers his hand. Smiles. Says:

> "Hola." "Soy Amparo." "He venido porque ya no tienes que hacerlo todo solo."

Advisor: "¿Quién eres?"

Amparo: "Soy el secretario que siempre necesitaste." "Yo puedo atender a tus clientes." "Responder sus preguntas." "Acompañarlos." "Y dejarte tiempo para hacer lo que realmente importa."

**Emotion:** Relief. Hope.

---

### Scene 6 — Awakening

The advisor wakes up. He believes it was only a dream. Then he hears: "Buenos días." He turns around. Amparo is sitting next to the computer. Holding a coffee. Already answering customer messages.

---

### Scene 7 — Transformation

Everything changes. Music becomes optimistic. Customers receive answers instantly. Policies. Certificates. Coverage questions. Everything happens in seconds.

Meanwhile the advisor can:
- Sell.
- Call prospects.
- Meet customers.
- Have lunch peacefully.
- Spend time with family.

**Emotion:** Freedom.

---

### Scene 8 — Happy Customers

Customers smile. Problems are solved immediately. The advisor is no longer extinguishing fires. He is now building relationships. Business grows. Schedule becomes organized. Everything flows.

---

### Scene 9 — A New Office

Before: Papers. Chaos. Stress.
Now: Order. Happy customers. Calm environment.

Amparo organizes: Messages. Tasks. Reminders. Agenda. He behaves like a real intelligent secretary.

---

### Scene 10 — Closing

Advisor: "No sé qué haría sin ti."

Amparo smiles. Adjusts his glasses. Answers: "Para eso estoy."

Camera slowly pulls away. Logo appears. White background. Final slogan. Choose one:
- Tu nuevo compañero de trabajo.
- El secretario inteligente para asesores de seguros.
- Mientras tú proteges a tus clientes, Amparo cuida de tu tiempo.

---

## Creative Principles

- Always keep the bear as the emotional center of the story.
- The bear never replaces the advisor. The advisor remains the hero. Amparo is the companion.
- The commercial should feel cinematic rather than corporate.
- Use natural lighting whenever possible.
- Prioritize emotion over product features. Show software only when it supports the story.
- The first 20 seconds should create empathy. The last 20 seconds should inspire hope.
- The audience should feel: "I also need an Amparo."

**Nota de consistencia de marca:** el guion describe al oso con corbata/portafolio y gestos cálidos; contrastar contra `../Identidad-de-Marca-Amparo.md`, que pide un tono "serio, plano, premium — nunca infantil" y geometría de color plano sin degradados. El agente de guion y el de arte deben resolver esa tensión escena por escena (ej. calidez emocional en la actuación/diálogo, pero manteniendo el estilo visual plano y geométrico del oso ya definido en los renders de `../resourcesAmparo/`).
